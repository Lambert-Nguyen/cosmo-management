Cosmo App � Developer Requirements Document (MVP / Must-Have Features)
1. User Roles & Permissions
* Admin/Manager
o Full control: create, assign, update tasks & schedules.
o Access to all reports, chat, and incident handling.
o Approve/review proof-of-work and inventory logs.
* Cleaning Staff
o Access only assigned cleaning & To-Do tasks.
o Complete checklists, upload photos, report shortages/damages.
o Log lost & found items.
* Maintenance Staff
o Access assigned maintenance + inventory tasks.
o Restock and log supply usage.
o Submit status updates, photos, and notes.
* Laundry Staff
o Access laundry-specific tasks (pick-up, wash/dry, drop-off, restock).
o Log shortages or damaged linens.
* Lawn/Pool Vendors
o Access assigned lawn/pool care tasks only.
o Submit proof-of-work (photos, GPS check-in/out).
* Property Owner/Viewer (optional future)
o Read-only view of reports and property readiness status.

2. Core Modules & Features
A. Cleaning Schedule Management
* Import daily cleaning schedules (check-in/check-out based).
* Calendar view of all cleaning tasks.
* Daily To-Do list for cleaners (push notification + in-app).
* Room-by-room checklists with blocking steps.
* Damage/Loss report field inside checklist.
* Lost & Found log with photo uploads.
* Proof of work: before/after photos, GPS + timestamp.

B. To-Do Management
* Admin can create additional daily tasks.
* Auto-updates staff To-Do list with notifications.
* Task statuses: Pending ? In Progress ? Completed.

C. Communication & Incident Handling
* In-app chat between Admin ? Cleaning staff, Admin ? Maintenance staff.
* Supports photos/videos for issue reporting.
* Urgent alerts for critical issues.
* Chat threads linked to property/task.

D. Image Upload & AI Quality Assurance
* Before/after photos required for cleaning.
* AI detects missed cleaning details (stains, residue, clutter).
* Generates automated quality alerts for Admin.
* Reports stored and linked to property & date.

E. Maintenance Schedule Management
* Admin creates/updates tasks (color-coded).
* Tasks assigned to staff or open to claim.
* Unified calendar view (with cleaning schedules).
* Notifications/reminders for due tasks.
* Statuses: Pending ? In Progress ? Completed.
* Weekly/monthly reports on property issues.

F. Supplies Inventory Management (NEW)
* Property Inventory Profiles
o Track consumables: toilet paper, soap, trash bags, light bulbs, batteries, AC filters, pool chemicals, etc.
o Define par levels per property.
* Stock-in / Stock-out Logging
o Maintenance logs items replenished/used.
o Cleaning staff can flag shortages ? auto-creates inventory task for maintenance.
* Alerts
o Low-stock notifications to Admin when levels drop below par.
o Urgent shortages flagged inside system.
* Proof & Audit
o Photo uploads for restocked items.
o Historical log of usage by date, property, and staff.
* Reporting
o Weekly/monthly supply usage reports.
o Exportable CSV/PDF for budgeting and owner reporting.

G. Laundry Management
* Task types: pick-up, wash/dry, drop-off, restock.
* Checklist: linens counted in/out, condition logged.
* Staff can mark shortages or damaged linens.
* Proof of work: photo upload for delivery/restock.
* Integrated with Inventory module for linen counts.

H. Lawn & Pool Care Management
* Recurring/one-time schedules for mowing, edging, fertilization, pool cleaning, chemical checks.
* Property-specific profiles (yard size, pool type, access notes).
* Proof of work: photos, GPS check-in/out.
* Alerts for overdue tasks or missed check-ins.
* Roles/permissions restricted by team.

3. Reporting & Oversight
* Cleaning Reports: task completion, SLA compliance.
* Maintenance Reports: weekly/monthly logs of issues & resolutions.
* Inventory Reports: usage, shortages, and restocking costs.
* Laundry Reports: linen counts, shortages, damages.
* Lawn/Pool Reports: completion rates, overdue alerts.
* Exports: CSV/PDF for admin and property owner reporting.

4. Notifications & Alerts
* Daily To-Do reminders for staff.
* Task reminders (24h before, 2h before start).
* Alerts for overdue/missed check-ins.
* Low-stock and shortage notifications.
* AI QA alerts for failed photo inspections.
* Urgent issue alerts from staff.

5. Acceptance Criteria (MVP �Definition of Done�)
* Admin uploads schedule ? auto-generates cleaning/maintenance/lawn/pool tasks.
* Staff get push notifications ? complete checklists & upload proof.
* Tasks cannot be marked complete without required checklists/photos.
* AI auto-checks cleaning photos ? alerts admin if issues detected.
* Damages/lost items reported with photo & logged.
* Cleaning shortages auto-generate inventory tasks ? maintenance team replenishes & logs.
* Weekly/monthly reports auto-generate & export.
* Unified calendar shows all tasks across teams.
* In-app chat supports staff-admin coordination with media sharing.

6. Technical Notes
* Mobile-first (Android/iOS with offline mode).
* Shared task engine & calendar across all modules.
* Photo compression + GPS/timestamp metadata.
* Push notifications for all role types.
* CSV/PDF reporting exports.
* AI module for photo QA
